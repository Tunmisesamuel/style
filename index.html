MY BEST FOOD

My best food is spag and sauce. To make my favorite meal is quite easy. You just need to get few things from the market. Check below for the list

  1. Golden penny spaghetti
  2. Tomato
  3. Rodo pepper
  4. Smoked Fish
  5. Egg
  6. Onions
  7. Groundnut oil
  8. Tomato paste (gino satchet)

Add a spoon of salt in a boiling water befiore adding your spag. Add a drop of vegetable oil, so the spag does not become sticky. When the spag is soft enough you have to remove the excess water so it does not become mashy. Get your pepper and tomato then grind it, make sure it is not too smooth. Then you get a pot and put vegetable oil it, make sure it is hot before adding sliced onions. Then add the pepper into the hot oil, and cover for some minutes befpore adding the perboiled egg.
